MORE FUN FACTS!

  • The Hulk was originally supposed to be gray! However, due to printing issues, the color kept coming out inconsistent — so Marvel changed his color to green, which quickly became iconic.

  • Batman's original design included red tights and blonde hair. It wasn’t until artist Bob Kane and writer Bill Finger revised the look that we got the dark, brooding Batman we know today.

  • The X-Men were created by Stan Lee and Jack Kirby as a way to simplify origin stories — their powers came from mutations, avoiding the need for accidents, magic, or alien technology.
